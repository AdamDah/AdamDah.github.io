The Bloomberg certification, also known as Bloomberg Market Concepts (BMC), is a course that provides an introduction to finance, utilizing the Bloomberg Terminal. AMF Certification

2023

The AMF certification, issued by the Autorité des Marchés Financiers (AMF), is a professional accreditation for individuals working in the financial services industry, particularly in France. This certification ensures that professionals possess the necessary knowledge and skills to comply with regulatory requirements and maintain high ethical standards. The AMF certification covers various topics related to financial markets, investment products, regulations, and risk management, enabling professionals to offer quality services and advice to their clients.
